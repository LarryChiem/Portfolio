About

Larry Chiem is currently a senior undergraduate in Computer Science at Portland State University.

He is in his second year interning at Intel on the High Performance Computing team where he's learned full-stack web development, automation, and scripting.

On his free time, Larry enjoys playing sports and working on sport projects to help inspire his community to playing sports.

He is currently working on a side project, Sport Spots, a website where any athlete, including disabled athletes, can find local gyms in Portland, Oregon, to play any sport, any day of the week. His future goals for this project is to expand the website to the world so that athletes* can empower themselves through sports.

*If you have a body you are an athlete.

His goal is to work in either Full Stack Web Development or Android Development at a company that invests in expanding human potential to their employees and to the whole world.
